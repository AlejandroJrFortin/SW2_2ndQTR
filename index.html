DOG BREEDS

Name Breed Description
Golden Retriever A sturdy, muscular dog of medium size, and known for its majestic looking hair. Also, they are
a family type of dog because they love playing and they are very sweet.
Alaskan Malamute Known for its strength and endurance to pull a sled and as well as its large size. They are basically
a type of dog that is a sled dog(in the arctic regions) and a hound (uses scent to hunt). Also, they are
similar to the husky.
American Bully This is a modern breed of dog which is used as a companion dog. This dog is know for its fierce look which
gives a "strong" impression. They are actually very happy, sweet, gentle, and loving to people.
They are very loyal to their owner and shows affection to their owner.
